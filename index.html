About the NGO

InAmigos Foundation is a registered non-profit organization committed to community development and social empowerment. It is Section 8 registered, 80G and 12A certified, CSR-1 registered, ISO 9001:2015 certified, and recognized by NGO Darpan - NITI Aayog. Founded by Mr. Govind Shukla on 23 September 2020.

BaseBilaspur, Chhattisgarh, India
FocusEducation, women empowerment, environment, animal welfare, and community service
StyleCommunity-driven, volunteer-led, and action-oriented

Its mission is bridging gaps in education, healthcare, and economic opportunity for underprivileged communities.
